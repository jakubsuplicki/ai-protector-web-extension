## What This Fork Changes

- Presents the project as a self-hosted browser-extension workflow.
- Keeps the runnable engine and browser extension together.
- Removes private planning and internal research material from the public repo.
- Starts public history as a curated import instead of publishing private
  workshop commits.

## Public History Policy

This public repository is intended to contain only public-facing commits. Private
research and planning remain in the private workspace. Public commits should be
created and pushed during the local working window configured in `.githooks/`.
